Cleaning

Effective cleaning and sanitizing procedures

Handwashing

Proper handwashing is essential for food safety.

Handwashing Steps

  1. Wet hands with warm running water
  2. Apply soap and rub hands together for at least 20 seconds
  3. Clean under fingernails and between fingers
  4. Rinse thoroughly with clean water
  5. Dry with clean towel or air dryer

Handwashing Facilities Checklist

Cleaning effectively

Follow the correct cleaning procedure to ensure surfaces are clean and safe.

Cleaning Steps

  1. Pre-clean - remove food debris
  2. Main clean - wash with hot soapy water
  3. Rinse - with clean hot water
  4. Disinfect - use approved disinfectant
  5. Final rinse - if required
  6. Dry - air dry or use clean cloth

Cooking safely

Cook food thoroughly to kill harmful bacteria and make food safe to eat.

Core Temperature Guide

Food should reach these core temperatures:

  • 75°C for 30 seconds, or
  • 70°C for 2 minutes, or
  • 65°C for 10 minutes, or
  • 60°C for 45 minutes

Reheating

Reheat food safely to kill any bacteria that may have grown.

Reheating Rules

  • Only reheat food once
  • Reheat to at least 75°C throughout
  • Reheat food as quickly as possible
  • Check temperature with a probe thermometer
